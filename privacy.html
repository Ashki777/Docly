Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Who we are

ClinicDesk is a student project developed as part of the Web Design & UX course at Technische Universität Berlin, 2026. The application is available at clinic-app-rouge.vercel.app.

2. What data we collect

When you use ClinicDesk, we may collect the following types of data: account information (name, email, clinic name), patient records entered by clinic staff, appointment and billing data, and basic usage information such as login times.

3. How we use your data

Data entered into ClinicDesk is used solely to provide the clinic management features described on this website. We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Data isolation

Each clinic's data is fully isolated. Staff members can only access data belonging to the clinic they have joined. No clinic can see another clinic's patients, appointments, or records.

5. Cookies

This website uses a cookie to remember your preference for dark mode and whether you have accepted this cookie notice. No tracking or advertising cookies are used.

6. Your rights under GDPR

If you are based in the European Union, you have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of any personal data we hold about you. To exercise these rights, contact us using the form on the contact page.

7. Data storage

Application data is stored via Vercel and associated cloud infrastructure. We take reasonable technical measures to protect data in transit and at rest.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The date at the top of this page will reflect the most recent update.
