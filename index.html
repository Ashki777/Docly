Patient Management

A complete profile for every patient

Every patient gets one unified profile with nine tabs — from allergies and chronic conditions to prescriptions, lab results, uploaded files, and billing. Everything in one place, no switching between systems.

  • Pre-clinic history: allergies, conditions, previous surgeries
  • Visit records and clinical notes per appointment
  • Prescriptions, lab orders, and file uploads
  • Billing records and payment tracking per patient

Insights

Understand how your clinic is performing

Appointment trends, revenue, patient growth, and status breakdowns — filtered by 3 months, 6 months, or a full year. No spreadsheets, no manual calculations.

  • Appointment trends over the last 12 months
  • Status breakdown: completed, cancelled, scheduled
  • Total revenue and outstanding balance at a glance
  • Patient count and visit frequency over time

Scheduling

Appointments that actually work

A calendar layout with a day view on the left and a schedule grid on the right. See the full day at a glance, book new patients on the spot, and check them in without any paperwork.

  • Search existing patients with autocomplete — auto-fills their details
  • Register new patients directly from the booking modal
  • Track appointment type, duration, and check-in time
  • Click any patient to open their full profile instantly

How it works

1

3
