What Docly does

Three tools built for everyday general practice.

Patient dashboard

See all appointments for the day, patient conditions, and any drug alerts before the first consultation starts.

Patient summary

Full medication list, symptom history, and past visit notes — pulled up automatically when you open a patient file.

Drug interaction checker

Type any two medications and Docly checks for known interactions using EMA-verified data. Alerts are colour-coded by severity.

How it works

Three steps and you are ready to go.

1

Connect your system

Docly connects to your existing patient management software. Setup takes about 20 minutes with our team.

2

Open the patient file

When a consultation starts, open the patient in Docly. Their history, medications, and alerts are already loaded.

3

Docly writes the notes

After each consultation, Docly generates a structured summary. Review it, approve it, move on.
