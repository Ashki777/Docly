Full feature documentation

Everything ClinicDesk can do, explained in plain language.

Clinic management

  • Doctors create a clinic through a guided onboarding flow
  • Staff join via invite codes generated by the clinic owner
  • Settings page to manage staff, update profile, and regenerate invite codes
  • Full data isolation — each clinic only sees its own data

Appointments

  • Calendar layout with a panel on the left and schedule grid on the right
  • Booking modal with patient search and autocomplete
  • Register new patients on the spot during booking
  • Tracks appointment type, duration, and check-in status
  • Click any appointment to go straight to the patient profile

Dashboard

  • Today's appointments at a glance
  • Total patients, unpaid invoices, monthly revenue
  • Quick actions: new appointment, new patient, new invoice
  • Clickable appointment cards that link to patient profiles

Patient profile — 9 tabs

Every patient has one unified profile. Here is what each tab contains.

1 — Overview

General patient information at a glance: name, date of birth, gender, blood type, contact details, and registration date.

2 — Pre-clinic history

Allergies with severity levels, chronic conditions, previous surgeries, past medications, and previous lab results. All entries are timestamped.

3 — Appointments

Full appointment history for that patient — dates, types, durations, and check-in status.

4 — Visit records

Clinical notes and documentation written by the doctor after each visit.

5 — Prescriptions

Medications prescribed per visit, with dosage and instructions.

6 — Labs

Lab orders and results, linked to the visit that requested them.

7 — Operations

Surgical operation forms and records for patients who have had procedures.

8 — Files

Upload and store scans, lab documents, referral letters, and any other patient files.

9 — Billing

Billing records and payment tracking per patient. Unpaid invoices surface on the dashboard.
